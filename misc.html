We offer exciting and educational talks to inspire the next generation, handling sessions to bring a smile to anyone’s face, or live training sessions to show what animals really can do.

Photography, Art & Filming

Looking for beautiful and amazing furry, scaly or feathery models? We are the right company for you.

We offer our animals for photography and art (classes, advertisement, portfolios), and filming (classes, films, advertisement, TV)

We have a wide range of animals you can choose from. All of them are very easy-going and used to being in the spotlight. They are very used to being handled and they have amazingly friendly natures.

We will travel to your place of choice and set up everything needed. If an animal is going to be handled, we will explain everything you need to know to do so safely.

Prices will depend on the job needed and the animal/s required.

Pet Removals

Are you moving to a new house and need to move all your pets? We can help you!

Our service includes:

  • Sorting out all your pet enclosures ready to travel safely (vivariums, tanks, sheds, pens etc.).
  • Making your pets comfortable and safe in their travel cages / boxes for the trip.
  • Transporting your animals in our van to your new home.
  • Rebuilding and preparing all their enclosures in their new home.
  • Cleaning the enclosures.
  • Getting everyone set up back in their enclosures.

Our aim is to make this stressful time a bit easier for you and your pets, taking a job out of your mind, knowing that we are experienced keepers with vast knowledge in enclosure set up and transporting animals safely.
