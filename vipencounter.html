Bringing Learning To Life

VIP Personal Animal Encounter

Are you an animal lover or do you know anyone who is an animal lover? Do you want to learn about a certain animal/s? Do you just want to spend some time with animals?

Book an animal experience with us. Choose from our Birds, Reptiles, Inverts and Mammal Experiences. We can offer a wide range of animal species, all very tame and easy-going to be around people of all ages and capabilities.

Check the “Our Animals” tab to see all the animals available.

As a VIP, you will be handling the animals and learning some amazing facts about them, and how to care for and handle them safely.

Our passion is to bring knowledge about animals and nature, sharing all our love for them with you, and educating everyone about the importance of taking care of our environment and protecting our planet and all the wildlife in it.

If you have any questions about our services, please contact us and find out what we can offer you.
