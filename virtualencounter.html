Bringing Learning To Life

Virtual Animal Encounters -
Putting the ZOO into ZOOm!

Enjoy extraordinary animal experiences from afar! Our experienced keepers will stream live from Exotic Explorers HQ and will share fascinating facts about our animals and answer any questions you have.

These Virtual Experiences can also give you the opportunity to watch feeding and witness our animal training techniques.

Choose from our Birds, Reptiles, Mammals, Invertebrates and Amphibians. Check the “Our Animals” tab to see all the animals available.

Our passion is to bring knowledge about animals and nature, sharing all our love for them with you, and educating everyone about the importance of taking care of our environment and protecting our planet and all the wildlife in it.

If you have any questions about our services, please contact us and find out what we can offer you.
