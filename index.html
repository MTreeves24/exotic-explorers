Bringing Learning To Life

Exotic Explorers is Animal Education that comes to you, catering for parties, schools, corporate events and more.

We are based in Essex and the company was set up by professional Zoo Keepers that have animal welfare and education at our hearts.

We aim to bring learning to life.

Services

Schools

Our school visits include exciting, educational talks, along with a chance to get up close and personal with some of our amazing animals.

Groups

Our groups and club visits are a exciting and entertaining way of getting close to our animals.

Parties

If you want a party that everyone will be talking about, look no further. Whether animal mad or looking to expand your knowledge on something new, we have the package for you.

SEN

Animals have a special way of bringing a smile to anyones day. We can visit care homes, therapy centers, SEN groups, hospices and more.

Outdoor Events/Shows

Looking for that special something to make your event stand out? We can Provide a Static Animal Display with a Marquee, animal handling sessions and educational talks

Personal Experiences

Choose from our Owls, Reptiles, Inverts and Furry experiences. Learn amazing facts about these beautiful animals, how to care for them and how to handle them safely.
