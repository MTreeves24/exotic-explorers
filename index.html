Bringing Learning To Life

Animal Experiences in and around Essex

Exotic Explorers promise to offer you an animal experience you'll never forget. Choose from over 70 species of exotic animals including Owls, Hawks, Skunks, Snakes, Lizards and Tarantulas!

We cater for personal experiences, parties, schools, SEN, corporate events, fetes/events and more.

We are a mobile zoo, and our coverage areas include Essex, London and sometimes further out (See our FAQs for details).

We are based in Essex and the company was set up by professional and highly experienced Zoo Keepers that have animal welfare and education at our hearts.

We aim to bring learning to life.

Covid-19 Update

As the 2nd UK Lockdown has come into force, we are now very sorry to say that we are unable to offer any of our usual services during this period. As you can imagine this is devastating to a small business like ours with huge animal overheads, and still no government help, however we will of course comply as we have done with all the other regulations.

There are still many ways you can help out our small business:

  • Book an Animal Experience where we'll observe social distancing and government guidance.
  • Book a Virtual Experience to enjoy seeing some of our animals and asking any questions you may have.
  • Adopt one of our animal team with one of our Adoption Packages.
  • Donate via our JustGiving page.
  • Buy us something from our Amazon Wish List.
  • Donating fresh fruit and vegetables.
  • Sending us a personal donation (contact us).
