Bringing Learning To Life

Exotic Explorers is Animal Education that comes to you, catering for parties, schools, corporate events and more.

We are based in Essex and the company was set up by professional Zoo Keepers that have animal welfare and education at our hearts.

We aim to bring learning to life.

Covid-19 Update

As of the 4th July 2020 we are now able to offer services again, and we also have reduced non-contact services available for those wanting to be extra safe.

With the new “Rule of Six”, we will only be able to accept bookings for up to 4 people when taking place in private houses, as we are two handlers and we count in those six.

Services taking place outside private houses such as Schools, SEN Groups, Care Homes, Children's Groups, etc are not affected by the “Rule of Six” and therefore will still be able to carry on with Covid-Secure measures in place.

We have PPE in place and follow the guidelines of shielding, self-distancing and disinfecting hands. We also must ask that all safety measures are followed at any experience we attend.

This is still a very uncertain time and due to the majority of our large summer events being cancelled or postponed, if you can donate anything to help us through this very difficult time please email us or use our justgiving link: justgiving.com/exoticexplorers

Please make sure you are all staying safe and well.
